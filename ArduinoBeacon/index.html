Relaxing Places

Please move within range of the Relaxation Beacons.

Feel Your Feet

  • Stand on your feet.
  • Become aware of the contact between the feet and the ground.
  • Feel your feet and into the ground itself.
  • Feel your knees.
  • Feel your hips.
  • Feel your spine.
  • Feel your shoulders.
  • Feel your neck.
  • Feel your head.
  • Experience the contact between the ground and your head.

Feel Your Shoulders

  • Let your arms hang loose.
  • Become aware of your shoulders.
  • Slightly lift one shoulder and release it.
  • Let the shoulder come all the way down.
  • Lift the other shoulder and release it.
  • Alternate three more times on each side.
  • Watch the air expanding your body.

Feel Your Face

  • Become aware of your face.
  • Feel the forehead.
  • The eyebrows.
  • Eyes.
  • Nose.
  • Nostrils.
  • Air flowing through the nostrils.
  • Cheeks.
  • Jaw.
  • Lips.
  • Tongue.
